I was a member of the student union back in my college years (BDE Majeistic).

Campaign site

From a design made by the Artist of the team (Anys), given to me under the form of an Photoshop file, I extracted the assets and animated them inside a Flash animation.

I also developed a simple guest book in PHP for the need of this campaign site.

A limited version of the site is online here, though most of the externals links are broken and the videos are not available.

Technologies: Adobe Flash CS3, LAMP.

Online voting platform

One of the tradition from the School of Engineering I graduaded in, is the election of students in categories such as "dancer of the year" , "couple of the year", … The event takes place once a year and is called "Fintz d'Or", after the Dean's name ("Nesim Fintz").

For this event, I developed a Rich Internet Application using Adobe Flex and REST web-services to power the UI. Everything: from the list of categories to auto-completion of the students' name was made dynamically using WS calls.

Once again, the design was based on a concept provided by our Artist (Anys).

A mock-up  is available online here.

Technologies: Adobe Flex, LAMP.
